Gut Feelings

Gut Feelings is a collaborative performance and installation project by Ewan James Knight and Nam Joo Huh, exploring the involuntary sounds and subtle gestures our bodies make — the ones we’re taught to suppress.

Combining ambient sound, performance lecture, spoken word, video, and visual art, Gut Feelings draws attention to the ordinary yet often unspoken experiences of sonic shame, care, and embodied memory. Ewan’s improvised audio — built from recordings of internal body sounds — shifts the atmosphere of the room, while Nam’s fragmented text and performance lecture trace intimate experiences of containment and release.

The work has been performed and exhibited at venues including BIRCH Studios & Gallery, Eastside Projects, and Riverside Studios. Contributions to the installation have also included sculpture by Lara Jennings and video by Ryu Kim.
