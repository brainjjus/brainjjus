Things Fall Between

Things Fall Between is an immersive, long-form ambient performance by Ewan James Knight, originally developed as his MA final project. The piece is structured in six movements, performed with guitar, effects, synthesizers, and electronics.

Premiered in June 2025, the work explores the sensation of falling — as a metaphor for psychological rupture — and uses layered textures, field recordings, and spatial audio to create an enveloping sound world. A studio version is set for release as a full album later in 2025.
