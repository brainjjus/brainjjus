About

Ewan Knight is a musician, producer, and educator based in Sheffield, specialising in immersive sound design and richly textured composition. His work includes soundtracks for independent games and collaborations across a range of genres. As brainjjus, his solo electronic project, he blends bio-music techniques, multiple instruments, and digital tools to craft atmospheric and emotionally resonant soundscapes. His debut immersive piece, Things Fall Between, premiered in June 2025 and will be released as a full-length album later this year. His debut EP, you might never be in the same room you are listening to this again, is also due for release in late 2025.
