About

Ewan Knight is a musician, producer, and educator based in Sheffield, specialising in immersive sound design and richly textured composition. His recent work includes scoring the independent game I Dancem Refusant (2024) and its forthcoming sequel, set for release in 2025. He also contributed as a songwriter, producer, and instrumentalist on SIM’s After a Bite (2025).

Under his solo electronic alias brainjjus, Ewan explores atmospheric soundscapes and bio-music techniques, blending organic and digital elements to create emotionally resonant works. His debut immersive piece, Things Fall Between, premieres on June 29th, 2025, with a digital release to follow later in the year.

Alongside his creative output, Ewan works as a session musician and offers music tuition in guitar, piano, and music theory.
