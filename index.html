About

Ewan Knight is a musician, producer, and educator based in Sheffield, specialising in immersive sound design and richly textured composition. His recent work includes composing original soundtracks for independent games and launching his solo electronic project brainjjus, which blends bio-music techniques, acoustic instrumentation, and digital processing to craft atmospheric and emotionally resonant soundscapes.

His debut immersive piece, Things Fall Between, premiered in June 2025 and will be released as a full-length album later this year. He is also preparing the release of his debut EP, you might never be in the same room you are listening to this again, set for release in late 2025.

Ewan is one half of the collaborative duo individual Hotpot with Nam Joo Hoo. Their experimental performance piece, Gut Feelings, explores themes of bodily tension, sonic discomfort, and involuntary expression through improvisation, ambient electronics, and manipulated vocal textures. The duo has presented the work in both academic and performance contexts.

In addition to his creative work, Ewan is available as a session musician and offers music tuition in guitar, piano, and music theory to learners of all ages and levels.
