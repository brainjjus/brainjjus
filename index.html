About

Ewan Knight is a musician, educator, and producer specialising in immersive sound design, based in Sheffield. His recent work includes composing for independent games, such as I Dancem Refusant (2024) and its upcoming sequel, set for release in 2025. He also contributed as a songwriter, producer, and instrumentalist on SIM’s After a Bite (2025). In addition to his collaborative projects, Ewan is launching his solo electronic project, brainjjus, with new music currently available on SoundCloud and the first singles set for distribution in April 2025. His work blends bio-music techniques and atmospheric sound design. A full-length immersive piece is set to premiere in July.
