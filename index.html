About

Ewan Knight is a Sheffield-based musician, producer, and educator specialising in immersive sound design. He has composed soundtracks for independent games and performs under the solo alias brainjjus, blending bio-music techniques and digital processing to craft atmospheric compositions. His debut immersive piece, Things Fall Between, premiered in June 2025 and will be released as a full-length album later this year. His debut EP, you might never be in the same room you are listening to this again, is also set for release in late 2025. In addition to his solo work, Ewan is active as a session musician and offers lessons in guitar, piano, and music theory.
