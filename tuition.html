Music Tuition

I offer one-to-one and small group lessons in a range of instruments and music subjects, tailored to all ages and experience levels. With over five years of teaching experience, I currently work with Sheffield Music Hub, Seven Hills Tuition, and Sheffield Inclusion Centre.

Lessons are available in:

  • 🎸 Classical, acoustic, and electric guitar (up to Grade 8 and beyond)
  • 🎹 Piano and keyboard
  • 🎵 Bass guitar, ukulele, and banjo
  • 🎧 Music theory and music production (Ableton Live, Pro Tools, etc.)

My teaching approach is flexible and student-centred — whether you're preparing for grades, developing original compositions, or simply learning for fun, I aim to make lessons engaging, supportive, and musically rich.
