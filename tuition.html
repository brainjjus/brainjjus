Music Tuition

Ewan is a qualified and dedicated music educator, offering lessons in classical, electric, and acoustic guitar (Grade 8 Classical), as well as bass guitar, keys, ukulele, banjo, music production, and music theory. He works with students of all ages and abilities, focusing on inclusive, supportive, and engaging tuition.

He currently teaches through Sheffield Inclusion Centre, Sheffield Music Hub, and Seven Hills Tuition. In-person lessons are available in Sheffield and surrounding areas.
