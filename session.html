Session Work

Ewan is an experienced and versatile session musician, comfortable on electric, acoustic, and classical guitar, as well as bass and keys. He works across a wide range of genres including rock, jazz, blues, pop, classical (guitar), funk, and soul. He is also available to support with mixing, mastering, and the creation of professional-quality backing tracks for solo performers.

He has worked with artists including Selina Lee, Mat Hook, Steel City Groovers, and Antifolk Hero. Rates vary depending on the scope of the project — please get in touch to discuss ideas and requirements.
